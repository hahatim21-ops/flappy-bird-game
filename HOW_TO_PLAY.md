## ðŸš€ Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Start the Server

The server should be starting now. Wait 10-15 seconds for it to compile.

### Step 2: Open the Game

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
2. Go to: **http://localhost:19006**
3. You should see the login screen

### Step 3: Create an Account

1. On the login screen, click **"Don't have an account? Sign up"**
2. Enter your email (e.g., `yourname@example.com`)
3. Enter a password (at least 6 characters, e.g., `password123`)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You should be automatically logged in and see the game!

### Step 4: Play the Game

1. Click **"Start"** button to begin
2. Click anywhere (or press spacebar) to make the bird flap
3. Avoid the pipes!
4. Try to get the highest score

### Step 5: Game Over

- When you hit a pipe, the game ends
- Click **"Restart"** to play again
- Your score is automatically saved to Supabase!
