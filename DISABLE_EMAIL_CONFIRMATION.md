# Disable Email Confirmation - Quick Fix

The email link expired. Let's disable email confirmation so you can login immediately.

## ✅ Step 1: Disable Email Confirmation in Supabase

1. Go to Supabase Dashboard: https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. Select your project: **"Flappy Bird Game"**
3. Click **"Authentication"** in the left sidebar
4. Click **"Providers"** tab
5. Click on **"Email"** provider
6. Find **"Confirm email"** toggle
7. Turn it **OFF** (gray)
8. Click **"Save"** at the bottom

## ✅ Step 2: Sign Up Again

1. Go back to your game: `http://localhost:19006`
2. Enter your email: `hsampalwala@gmail.com`
3. Enter a password (at least 6 characters)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You should be logged in immediately! ✅

## ✅ Step 3: Or Sign In If Account Exists

If you already created the account:

1. On the login screen, make sure you're on **"Sign in"** mode (not "Sign up")
2. Enter your email: `hsampalwala@gmail.com`
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Login"**
5. You should be logged in and see the game!
